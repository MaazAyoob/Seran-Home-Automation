Who We Are

Where Technology Meets Luxury

Seran was born from a simple belief: that the technology controlling your home should feel invisible, intuitive, and elegant. Our founders — a team of electrical engineers and interior design enthusiasts — saw a gap in Bengaluru's market for truly premium, bespoke automation experiences.

Starting with just three residential projects in Jayanagar, we grew steadily through word of mouth alone. Today we serve clients across Bengaluru — from Koramangala penthouses to Whitefield tech campuses to five-star hotels in Indiranagar.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values
